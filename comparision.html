Why Compare Tiles?

The comparison feature helps customers make better and more informed decisions by allowing them to view multiple tile options side by side in a clear and structured way. It highlights all the important details such as size, material, durability, pricing, and special advantages for each tile, making it easier to understand how each option differs from the others. Instead of checking each product individually, users can quickly compare all key features in one place, saving time and effort. This feature is designed to simplify the selection process, reduce confusion, and help customers confidently choose the tile that best matches their needs, preferences, and budget.
