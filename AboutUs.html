Who We Are

We are a passionate team of design innovators dedicated to transforming ordinary surfaces into extraordinary architectural statements. We source, curate, and deliver premium tiles that bridge the gap between structural utility and high-end interior fashion.

Our Collections

Our catalog features a diverse range of aesthetics, from sleek ultra-modern minimalist porcelain to intricate traditional ceramics. Each piece is selected for its superior durability, flawless finish, precision cuts, and unique texture profiles.

Why Choose Us

We combine eco-friendly manufacturing standards with cutting-edge digital printing technology. This ensures our scratch-resistant, water-impermeable tiles maintain vibrant colors and deep textures for decades under heavy foot traffic.

The Heritage

Rooted in engineering mastery, Casa Piedra honors architectural history by blending traditional Italian stonework philosophies with modern luxury lifestyles.

The Artistry

Every slab and tile profile in our gallery undergoes strict visual testing to guarantee deep tonal gradients and authentic tactile patterns.

Our Design Philosophy

We believe tiles are not just flooring elements; they are the canvas of your space. Whether you are seeking bold geometric patterns to make a statement, rustic stone finishes for warmth, or large-format slabs for seamless transitions, our designs are engineered to elevate modern living.
